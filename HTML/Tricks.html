Tricks


Ollie

This is an example of an ollie. It is a trick in which you bring your board off the ground. You can perform it by popping the the tail of the board and sliding your foot up towards the nose.




Kickflip

This is an example of an Kickflip. It is a trick similar to an ollie, but instead of only bringing your board of the ground you also make it flip. You can perform it by popping the the tail of the board and sliding your foot up towards the nose and either to the right or left depending on how you ride.
