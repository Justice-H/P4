Maintenance


Its important to maintain you skateboard because the more your ride the quicker it wears out so by caring for it properly you can make it last longer. So here are the do's and dont's.

Do's:

  • Clean your bearings
  • Check your bolts
  • Check your trucks

Dont's:

  • Ride in the rain
  • leave your board in the sun
  • leave your board in a humid place

Besides some of the do's and dont's there are many other things you can do. Sometimes you have to buy new parts for you skateboard and some prefer this for cheaper parts like bearings. But its possible to clean them with some rubbing alcohol; you want to avoid using water for things like this. There can also come a point where your wheels can become to worn so you might have to buy new ones.
